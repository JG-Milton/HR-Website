At the heart of Jaya Grocer, the spirit of togetherness and community thrives.

   Jaya grocer isn't just a grocery store; it's a place where people come together to share their love for quality products and genuine connections.

We love our people and we call ourselves JGs!

   Established in 2008 by the Teng Family, Jaya Grocer has grown to become the leading mass-premium supermarket chain in Malaysia. Jaya Grocer prioritizes freshness and quality, offering consumers an excellent selection of quality of fresh produce and a wide range of grocery items from international and local brands, alongside affordable staples.

 From the launch of it's first store in Plaza 33 Petaling Jaya, the brand now operates over 50 stores today, with the majority located in Klang Valley. It's continued expansion over the years is a testament to the demand of Jaya Grocer's distinctive grocery concept. Jaya Grocer's commitment is to curate grocery concepts based on it's location, providing a pleasant shopping experience with modern store layouts, well-organized premises and an emphasis on customer experience. The inclusion of specialty sections catering to specific dietary needs or preferences further enhances it's appeal to a diverse customer base.



 The integration of online shopping into Jaya Grocer through a synergistic partnership with Grab signified a pivotal aspect of it's business strategy. It is aimed to make on-demand groceries more accessible to a broader audience. The ongoing collaboration is poised to ride the next wave of app-based solutions, facilitating a seamless and convenient shopping experience from the comfort of one's home. This strategic alignment with technological advancements underscores our commitment to staying at the forefront of innovation in the grocery retail sector.

 Jaya Grocer's VISION is to become the leading ONLINE-TO-OFFLINE (O2O) Retailer, leveraging omnichannel strengths to provide a REWARDING, FULFILLING and EXCITING experience for customers. Jaya Grocer's MISSION is to provide a diverse variety of high-quality and fresh produce at attractive best value prices, ensuring our customers have the best shopping experiences. We are commited to growing our business through successful collaboration and partnerships, while practicing the core VALUES of Jaya Grocer every single day; Growth, Trust, Teamwork, Customer Satisfaction and collaboration and partnerships while practicing the core VALUES of Jaya Grocer every single day; Growth, Trust, Teamwork, Customer Satisfaction and Integrity.

 The expansion of Jaya Grocer's presence across Malaysia with outlets strategically located in prime areas including shopping malls and standalone stores reflects it's ambition to serve a wider audience. Through it's focus on people, quality, variety and customer satisfaction, Jaya Grocer has successfully established itself as a trusted name in the Malaysian retail market.
